Track System Analytics and Feedback

Analytics and feedback are critical to the success of your business

Our Real Estate apps, for example, track how many people visited the home, how long they spent there, and whether they were interested or not. While this information is of great interest to home sellers, it can be invaluable for builders and property developers. They can now see how visitors move through the property and which rooms or features create the most interest for the longest time. This information allows developers and architects to design homes ideally suited for the needs of people who will live there in the future.
